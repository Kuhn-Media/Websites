Appointments & First Visits

Please bring a photo ID, any dental insurance information you have, and a list of any medications you are currently taking. This will help us provide you with the most comprehensive care.

A typical new patient exam and cleaning takes about 60-90 minutes. We take our time to be thorough and to answer all of your questions without rushing.

We kindly request at least 48 hours' notice if you need to reschedule an appointment. This allows us to offer the time slot to another patient in need of care.

Treatments & Procedures

Yes, we offer professional teeth whitening services that provide safer and more effective results than over-the-counter products. Please ask us about your options during your consultation.

Dental implants are a permanent solution for replacing missing teeth. A titanium post acts as a new root, and a custom crown is placed on top, restoring the look, feel, and function of a natural tooth.

Pediatric Dentistry

Absolutely. We love welcoming children to our practice and have a special focus on making their dental care a positive and fun experience.

Just like adults, children should have a routine checkup and cleaning every six months. This helps us monitor their development and apply preventive treatments to keep their smiles healthy.
