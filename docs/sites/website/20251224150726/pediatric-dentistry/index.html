The American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry recommends a first visit around your child's first birthday. This allows us to ensure their dental development is on track and to provide you with important oral care guidance.

You can help by maintaining a positive attitude. Read books or watch videos about visiting the dentist. We'll handle the rest by explaining everything in simple, child-friendly terms.
