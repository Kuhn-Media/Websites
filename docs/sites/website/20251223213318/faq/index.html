Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our practice, treatments, and patient care.

Appointments & Your Visit

Please bring a photo ID, any dental insurance information you may have, and a list of any medications you are currently taking. Arriving a few minutes early can help ensure all paperwork is completed without rushing.

A typical new patient exam and cleaning takes about 60 to 90 minutes. This allows us enough time for a thorough examination and to discuss our findings with you without feeling rushed.

We kindly request at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the time slot to another patient in need of care.

Children's Dentistry

Absolutely! We love welcoming new families and children to our practice. Our team is specially trained to make dental visits a positive experience for our youngest patients.

Yes, parents are welcome to accompany their children during their appointment. We find that your supportive presence can be very comforting, especially during their first few visits.

Treatments & Procedures
